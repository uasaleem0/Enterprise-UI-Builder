---USER_FLOWS---
## Onboarding Flow
1. User lands on homepage
2. Clicks "Get Started"
3. Creates account (email/password)
4. Completes profile setup
5. Arrives at dashboard

## Workout Tracking Flow
1. User navigates to Workouts
2. Selects or creates workout
3. Logs exercises and sets
4. Saves workout
5. Views updated progress
